Types of spa and their places




1.Mty Massage:

Set in a small urban setting, Myth Massage Spa offers affordable massages which strictly adhere to the use of Traditional Thai natural herbal products. Various massages ranging from simple foot and head massages to shoulder and full-body massages, all the way up to aromatherapy and deep tissue massages are offered with professional masseurs on hand. Add on services such as herbal compression, body scrubs and even fortune reading are available.

Address: 28/3 Soi Ruam Ruedi, Lumphini, Pathum Wan District, Bangkok 10330
Timings: 12:00 PM to 12:00 AM

2. Darin Beauty Salon and Massage Spa:

Darin Beauty Salon and Massage Spa in Bangkok has been active in the business for over 20 years, and they are very well-known for providing great quality service at affordable rates to their customers. Along with being a spa, it also has a beauty salon which provides high-class manicures and pedicures along with waxing, eyelash extensions and everything else. A wide range of massages including the famous aromatherapy to herbal compresses are offered at affordable rates.

Address: 303 Rambutree Rd, Talad yod, Phranakorn, Bangkok 10200
Timings: 8:00 AM to 11:30 PM

3.Yunomori Onsen and Spa:

Yunomori Onsen & Spa in Bangkok brings Japanese practices of physical and mental wellness straight to the streets of the capital. Here, they use thermal spring water and natural minerals from Wat Wangkanai to replicate the ancient Japanese onsen bathing culture. This practice is believed to rejuvenate and relax the skin. Their traditional Thai massage techniques are also very popular. Besides the traditional Thai massage services, you can also try their special and self-made aromatherapy, facial treatments, and herbal compresses.

Address: 120/5 Sukhumvit 26 Alley, Khwaeng Khlong Tan, Khet Khlong Toei, Krung Thep Maha Nakhon 10110
Timings: 9:00 AM to 12:00 AM

4.Baan Dalah Mind and Body Spa:

Recommended by both locals and tourists, Baan Dalah Mind and Body Spa is a spa that offers amazing spa services. Awarded a Certificate of Excellence from TripAdvisor in 2013, the quality of massages here has been compared to high-end luxury spas and health centres. At Baan Dalah Mind and Body Spa, a full range of different Thai massage and spa therapies along with casual body scrubs, waxing services, and facials.

Address: 2 Baan Dalah Mindbody Spa. Sukhumvit Soi.8, Sukhumvit Road, Khlong Toey, Bangkok 10110
Timings: 10 AM to Midnight

5.Urban Retreat Spa:

Urban Retreat Spa in Bangkok is a luxurious massage and day spa that provides customers with a professional, affordable, and clean experience. The spa places emphasis on the quality of its professional staff, spa products, and cleanliness, ensuring that supplies are ordered in smaller batches to guarantee freshness. Herbal balls are never reused, and fresh towels are provided for every guest. The spa is easily accessible by public transportation and offers a serene and tranquil environment for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

Address: 348, 1 Sukhumvit Road, Khlong Toei Nuea, Watthana, Bangkok 10110, Thailand Timings: Opens daily, 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM
